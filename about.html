ABOUT US

While we may not be onboard NASA's missions to outer space, we have a space mission of our own: educating the next generation about humanity's final frontier. We believe that when we encourage humans' natural instincts of curiousity, collaboration, and creativity, we can turn science fiction into reality. In less than 5 centuries, we've gone from believing that Earth was the center of the universe to discovering planets beyond even our own solar system. Who knows that the future millennia will hold?
